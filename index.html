About me

I work as a Research Associate at School of Psychology and Neuroscience, University of Glasgow (UofG), UK.

Previously, I worked as a Research Associate at Biosciences Institute, Newcastle University (NCL), UK. I have worked at Wellcome Centre for Human Neuroimaging (WCHN), University College London (UCL), UK. I have also worked as a Senior Engineer in Media Processing - Audio team, for Ittiam Systems, Bangalore, India.

I earned my PhD in auditory perception and Masters in Neuroscience from Institute of Neuroscience (IoN), Newcastle University (NCL), UK. I obtained my Bachelors degree in Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE) from National Institute of Technology Trichy (NITT), India. I am a recipient of Wellcome Trust PhD studentship, NCL Uni Overseas Research Scholarship.
