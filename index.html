About me

I work as a Research Associate at Biosciences Institute, Newcastle University (NCL), UK. Previously, I have worked at Wellcome Centre for Human Neuroimaging (WCHN), University College London (UCL), UK. I have also worked as a Senior Engineer in Media Processing - Audio team, for Ittiam Systems, Bangalore, India. I earned my PhD in auditory perception and Masters in Neuroscience from Institute of Neuroscience (IoN), Newcastle University (NCL), UK. I obtained my Bachelors degree in Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE) from National Institute of Technology Trichy (NITT), India. I am a recipient of Wellcome Trust PhD studentship, and Overseas Research Scholarship from Newcastle University. I am a TATA scholar.
